# Privacy Policy / Datenschutzerklärung

**gregFramework - Wiki & Mod Store**

**Last updated / Stand: 24. April 2026**

---

## English Version

### 1. Data Controller
**Meyer Media**  
Owner: Marvin Meyer  
Email: privacy@gregframework.local

### 2. Data We Process
- **Account data**: Username, email, password (hashed), profile information (optional).
- **Usage data**: Pages visited, downloads, interactions, IP address, browser type.
- **Content**: Mods, documentation, comments, and other assets you upload.
- **Transactions**: Billing address and payment history for paid services.

### 3. Purposes of Processing
- Providing and managing your account and our services.
- Analyzing usage to optimize and improve the platform.
- Ensuring security and preventing abuse (spam, fraud, attacks).
- Communicating with you regarding updates or support.
- Complying with legal and tax obligations.

### 4. Legal Basis (GDPR)
- **Consent** (Art. 6(1)(a)): Marketing, non-essential cookies.
- **Contract performance** (Art. 6(1)(b)): Providing the services you signed up for.
- **Legitimate interests** (Art. 6(1)(f)): Security, analytics, fraud prevention.
- **Legal obligation** (Art. 6(1)(c)): Tax and accounting requirements.

### 5. Your Rights
Under GDPR, you have the following rights:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data.
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure**: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten").
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data.
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Withdrawal**: Withdraw consent at any time.

**Supervisory Authority**:  
Bundesbeauftragter für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit (BfDI)  
Website: [https://www.bfdi.bund.de/](https://www.bfdi.bund.de/)

### 6. Security
We implement robust security measures:
- TLS/SSL encryption for all data transfers.
- Strong password hashing (bcrypt/argon2).
- Regular security updates and firewalls.
- Access controls on internal systems.

- **Einwilligung** (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. a): Marketing, optionale Cookies.
- **Vertragserfüllung** (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. b): Bereitstellung der Dienste.
- **Berechtigte Interessen** (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. f): Sicherheit, Analyse, Optimierung.
- **Gesetzliche Verpflichtung** (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. c): Steuerliche Dokumentation.

### 5. Ihre Rechte
Sie haben folgende Rechte gemäß DSGVO:
- **Auskunft**: Welche Daten wir über Sie verarbeiten.
- **Berichtigung**: Korrektur unrichtiger Daten.
- **Löschung**: Recht auf Vergessenwerden.
- **Einschränkung**: Verarbeitung einschränken lassen.
- **Datenübertragbarkeit**: Daten in gängigem Format erhalten.
- **Widerspruch**: Gegen Verarbeitung auf Basis berechtigter Interessen.
