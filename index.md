## Why gregCore v2.0?

Version 2.0 fundamentally modernized the framework:
- **Unified API**: Access all game functions through the central `GregAPI`.
- **Multi-Language**: Write mods in your favorite language.
- **Event-Driven**: A stable, ID-based event system for real-time interaction.
