# 🏗️ Content Creation

gregCore allows you to extend the game's world with your own content. By using our **Registries**, your items will behave just like vanilla game objects, including persistence and UI support.

## Major Content Types

### 🖥️ [Custom Servers](/guides/content/custom-servers)
Define new server chassis, specify internal slot counts, and set power consumption profiles.

### 🔌 [Custom Switches](/guides/content/custom-switches)
Create high-density networking gear with custom port layouts and routing capabilities.

### 👥 [NPCs & Employees](/guides/content/custom-npcs)
Add new staff types with unique traits, skills, and salaries. Integrate them into the HR system.

### 🤝 [Customers & Contracts](/guides/content/custom-customers)
Design new customer archetypes with specific SLA requirements, budget constraints, and churn logic.

---

::: tip
**Persistence**: All custom content registered through gregCore is automatically saved and loaded alongside the vanilla savegames.
:::
