# Getting Started

Welcome to the technical hub for developing mods and plugins for **Data Center**. This documentation is the central point of contact for anyone who wants to expand the gaming experience or improve the **gregCore** framework.

> ### Focus: Data Center by Waseku
> This wiki content is specifically tailored to **Data Center** and is not intended as a general Unity modding guide.

## Your First Steps

1. **[Quickstart Guide](/getting-started/quickstart)** – Choose your language (C#, Rust, Lua) and create your first mod in record time.
2. **[System Architecture](/getting-started/architecture)** – Learn about the structure of the framework and the role of the **GregAPI**.
3. **[Hooks & Events](/api/hooks/index)** – Understand how you can react to game events in real-time.

## Choosing Your Language

Since gregCore v2.0, you have the freedom to choose the tool that best fits your project:

- **C# (Managed)**: For complex framework extensions and deep IL2CPP integration.
- **Rust (Native)**: For computationally intensive logic and maximum stability through the FFI system.
- **Lua (Scripting)**: For rapid prototyping, UI optimization, and simple gameplay changes.
