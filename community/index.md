# Community Hub

The **gregFramework** is built for and powered by the community. This hub provides resources for connecting with other developers, finding support, and contributing to the ecosystem.

---

## 🚀 Why Join the gregCore Ecosystem?

If you are an existing *Data Center* modder, migrating to the **gregCore** framework offers significant advantages:

- **Automatic Settings UI**: No need to build custom menus; your settings appear in the F8 Hub.
- **Conflict Resolution**: The framework prevents keybind and resource overlaps between mods.
- **Save Stability**: Integrated persistence services ensure your data survives game updates and IL2CPP stripping.
- **Enhanced Visibility**: Your mod is automatically listed in the official in-game browser.

## 🤝 Getting Involved
- **[Discord](https://discord.gg/greg)**: The central place for real-time support, code sharing, and development updates.
- **[GitHub](https://github.com/mleem97/gregCore)**: Report bugs, suggest features, and contribute to the source code.
- **[Ecosystem Sponsors](./sponsors)**: Recognition for those who financially support our infrastructure.

## 🛠️ Safe Patching Standards
Maintaining a stable environment requires all mods to follow shared standards for method interception and data manipulation. Refer to the **[Safe Patching Standards](./safe-patching-standards)** for detailed rules.
